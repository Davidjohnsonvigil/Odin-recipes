Chipotle Chicken

Description

This dish is something I personally love. Whenever it is on the menu I am automatically picking this delicious chicken. It is perfect in a sandwich or in a burrito. The prep time is 10 minutes while the cook time is 5 minutes. Any additional time for other prefered variants can add an additional 18 hours to the cooking time. This will serve 8 people. This dish contains 293 Calories, 19g fat, 6g carbs, and 25g of protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce of dried chipotle chile pepper
  • 1 ounce dried ancho chile pepper
  • Half cup of water
  • Half red onion, cut into small chunks
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 and a half pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs

Steps

  1. Step 1 Place chipotle and ancho chile peppers in a shallow bowl; Pour in water.Cover the bowl and let sit at room tempature until peppers are softened, 10 to 12 hours. Drain water and remove seeds from peppers.
  2. Step 2 Blend chile peppers, red onion, garlic, cumin, oregano, and black pepper in a blender until a coarse paste forms; add olive oil and blend until marinade is smooth.
  3. Step 3 Place chicken thighs between 2 sheets of heavy plastic on a solid, level surface. Firmly pound chicken with the smooth side of a meat mallet to a half inch thickness.
  4. Step 4 Place chicken in a resealable plastic bag and add marinade. Turn chikcen several times to coat. Seal bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours.
  5. Step 5 Preheat an indoor grill with top and bottom plates for medium-high heat.
  6. Step 6 Remove chicken from the bag and discard marinade.
  7. Step 7 Place chicken on the preheated grill,close the lid, and cook until no linger pink in the center and the juices run clear, 5 to 7 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read atleast 165 degrees F (74 degrees C). Cut chicken into strips and serve warm.
